# Campus Bus Schedule

There are two types of campus buses: staff buses and student buses. Staff buses give priority to faculty and staff. Student buses currently only run between Wushan and the International Campus.

Below is a reference table for campus bus stop times between campuses for the Spring 2026 semester. Specific arrangements are subject to the latest official notice. Unless otherwise marked, the default is staff bus.

Note: For the International Campus, please make a reservation through the **Smart Services** mini-program and contact the driver.
