# Overseas Master's Programs

Introduction to overseas master's programs and tips for applying to graduate programs abroad from a SCUT undergraduate background.

## Hong Kong Study Programs

Below is a summary of the key differences between **MSc, MPhil, and PhD** in the Hong Kong higher education system, covering **duration, tuition, academic pathways, academic focus, and career orientation**.

***

### Core Comparison Table

| **Program**                 | **MSc (Taught Master's)**                   | **MPhil (Research Master's)**                    | **PhD (Research Doctorate)**                          |
| --------------------------- | ------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Duration**                | 1 year (full-time)                          | 2 years (full-time)                              | 4-5 years (full-time)                                 |
| **Tuition (annual)**        | **12-24万 HKD** (25-35万 RMB) (self-funded) | **4.2万 HKD** (subsidized)                       | **4.2万 HKD** (subsidized)                            |
| **Scholarship**             | Limited merit-based scholarships            | Yes **HKPFS/Professor funding** (~18万 HKD/year) | Yes **HKPFS/full scholarship** (~25万 HKD/year)       |
| **Academic Format**         | Courses + exams/thesis                      | Research-focused + few courses                   | **Independent research + academic thesis**            |
| **Career Path**             | Employment                                  | Stepping stone to PhD                            | Academia/research/high-end industry R\&D               |
| **Graduation Requirements** | Complete credits + pass thesis              | Publish paper + thesis defense                   | **International journal papers + PhD thesis defense** |
| **Application Difficulty**  | Moderate (GPA + language scores)            | High (research proposal + supervisor networking) | Very high (top research output + matching supervisor) |

***

### Academic Pathways and Progression

#### MSc to PhD?

* **Extremely difficult**: MSc lacks systematic research training; requires additional research experience (e.g., published papers) for PhD applications.

#### MPhil to PhD

* After the first year, pass the **transfer assessment** to directly enter the PhD phase (total duration: 2+2=4 years).

#### Direct-entry PhD from Undergraduate

* **Requirements**: Top 985 university (GPA >= 3.7) + research papers (first author) + strong recommendation letters. **(Strong recommendation ≈ research papers >> GPA)**
* **Advantage**: Lowest total cost (scholarship covers the entire period).

> ⚠️ **Heads-up**: Undergraduates without research experience applying for MPhil/PhD face significant difficulty. It's strongly recommended to choose an MSc as a buffer!
